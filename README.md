📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes financial data to understand revenue trends, profitability drivers, and overall business performance.

The solution combines data modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive dashboards to simulate a real-world business reporting system.

Key Components:

- Data cleaning and transformation  
- Star schema data modeling  
- KPI development (Revenue, Profit, Profit Margin, YoY Growth)  
- Multi-dimensional financial analysis  
- Interactive Power BI dashboards  
- Business insights and recommendations  

The objective is to help stakeholders monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

📂 Dataset

The dataset contains financial and sales data:

- Date → Transaction date  
- Product → Product category  
- Segment → Customer segment  
- Country → Geographic region  
- Sales → Revenue generated  
- Profit → Profit earned  
- Discount → Discount applied  
- COGS → Cost of goods sold  
- Units Sold → Quantity sold

❓ Business Questions Answered

- How is revenue trending over time?  
- Which products generate the highest profit?  
- Which countries contribute most to profit?  
- What is the overall profit margin?  
- How does performance change year-over-year?  

---

⚙️ Data Modeling

- Built a star schema model  
- Fact table → Financials  
- Dimension tables → Date, Product, Segment, Country  

---

🔎 Key Insights

- Revenue shows strong growth in Q4  
- Paseo is the highest revenue-generating product  
- France contributes the highest profit  
- Profit margins vary across countries  
- Certain segments drive higher revenue  

---

💡 Business Recommendations

- Focus on high-performing products (Paseo)  
- Improve performance in low-margin regions  
- Optimize discount strategies to increase profitability  
- Target high-revenue customer segments
